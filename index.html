Lewis Barrett

I’m a Computing graduate from Donegal, Ireland, with hands-on experience across IT support, software development, systems administration, and cloud technologies. My background includes troubleshooting, hardware repair, Linux environments, AWS, and building practical software solutions that solve real-world problems.

I’ve worked in fast-paced technical environments including healthcare IT, supporting systems and end users, while also developing personal projects focused on automation, scripting, data handling, and web-based applications. I enjoy turning technical knowledge into practical results, whether that means solving problems, improving systems, or building something useful from the ground up.

Alongside my professional and personal projects, I also volunteer in a local community-focused technology repair environment. This gives me practical experience working with computers, troubleshooting issues, setting up systems, and helping extend the life of older devices through repair and reuse.

A big part of this site is sharing what I learn. Through my tutorials and notes, I want to help other people develop their skills, understand technical concepts more clearly, and grow with me as I continue learning myself.

If you are looking for someone to work with, need technical help, want to collaborate on a project, or simply want to get in touch, feel free to use the contact page. I’m always open to IT support, junior cloud or systems work, software projects, or knowledge sharing.
